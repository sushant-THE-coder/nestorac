Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to list my property?

Listing your property is completely free. We only charge a 3% commission fee when you successfully rent your property through our platform, which is significantly lower than traditional real estate agents who typically charge 8-12%.

How are tenants screened?

All potential tenants undergo a thorough screening process that includes credit checks, employment verification, rental history, and criminal background checks. You'll have access to all this information before approving any tenant.

What if there's damage to my property?

Our lease agreements include a damage protection clause that covers up to $1 million in damages. We also collect a security deposit from tenants (typically equal to one month's rent) that can be used to cover any damages or unpaid rent.

How do I receive rent payments?

Rent is collected electronically from tenants each month and automatically deposited into your bank account, minus our small commission fee. You'll receive payment on the same day each month.

Can I set rules for my property?

Absolutely. You can specify house rules regarding smoking, pets, guests, quiet hours, and other preferences. These rules will be included in the lease agreement and communicated clearly to tenants.
